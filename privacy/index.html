FUSE Privacy Policy

This policy explains how FUSE handles information when you play the iOS game. FUSE is designed as a local-first mobile game: gameplay progress, settings, themes, remembered cascades, and Daily Ignition history are stored on your device unless a platform service is needed for a feature you choose to use.

Information FUSE Stores On Your Device

  • Campaign progress, scores, tap history, chain counts, and achievements.
  • Settings such as audio, haptics, theme, reminders, and language.
  • Daily Ignition results, local replay data, and optional share text.
  • Purchase entitlement state so paid features can unlock in the game.
  • Local monetization events used to understand ads, offers, and purchases.

Advertising And Consent

FUSE may offer optional rewarded ads through Google AdMob. Ads are not requested during first-run onboarding. Where required, Google UMP presents a consent dialog before ad requests. If consent is limited, FUSE requests non-personalized ads.

Apple App Tracking Transparency may ask whether you allow tracking for advertising purposes. If you decline, FUSE does not use the advertising identifier for tracking.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple StoreKit. FUSE does not receive your payment card, Apple ID password, billing address, or bank details. Purchase state is stored locally so FUSE Complete and related entitlements can be restored in the game.

Analytics

FUSE buffers monetization and gameplay interaction events locally, such as offer views, purchase outcomes, consent status, rewarded ad outcomes, and theme selections. If a production analytics endpoint is configured, those events may be sent to help improve balancing and reliability. Analytics failures never block gameplay.

Sharing

Daily Ignition can use the iOS share sheet. Sharing is optional. The system only shares the text or link you choose to send.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple StoreKit for purchases and restore.
  • Google AdMob for optional rewarded ads.
  • Google User Messaging Platform for consent where required.
  • Apple App Tracking Transparency for advertising permission.
  • iOS share and local notification services for optional features.

What FUSE Does Not Collect

  • FUSE does not ask for your name, phone number, camera, microphone, photos, contacts, or precise location.
  • FUSE does not sell personal information.
  • FUSE does not require an account to play.

Your Choices

  • Use iOS Settings to change App Tracking Transparency choices.
  • Use the in-game Settings screen to restore purchases and access ad privacy choices when available.
  • Delete the app to remove local gameplay data from your device.
  • Contact support for privacy questions or data requests.
