FUSE — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This privacy policy describes how FUSE ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application on iOS.

Information We Collect

Automatically collected

  • Device information — device model, iOS version, locale, timezone, and approximate region (for ad serving and consent determination).
  • Usage data — anonymous gameplay metrics (levels completed, session length, chain lengths) stored locally and used to improve the game.
  • Ad interactions — when you tap "Watch for Nova Pulse" and view an optional rewarded ad, we record whether the ad completed. We do not record ad content.
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you choose "Allow" on Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline, no IDFA is read or shared.

Provided by you

  • In-app purchases — processed entirely by Apple's StoreKit. Purchase confirmations are validated on your device; we never see your payment details, billing address, or Apple ID.

We do NOT collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or other personal identifiers.
  • Your precise location.
  • Your photos, contacts, microphone, or camera.
  • Any financial account data, credit card numbers, or bank details.
  • Any health or biometric data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To run the game on your device (save progress, apply your chosen theme and signature style).
  • To serve rewarded advertisements through Google AdMob, if you have consented and have not purchased FUSE Complete.
  • To improve the game by reviewing anonymous aggregate usage metrics.
  • To process in-app purchases through Apple StoreKit.

Third-Party Services

FUSE integrates with the following third parties, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — serves optional rewarded advertisements. See Google's privacy policy.
  • Google UMP (User Messaging Platform) — presents the consent dialog used in the EU, UK, Switzerland, and applicable US states.
  • Apple StoreKit — processes all in-app purchases. See Apple's privacy policy.
  • Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — controls whether your advertising identifier (IDFA) is shared with ad partners.

FUSE does not use Game Center, iCloud sync, Facebook SDK, Firebase, or any social-media integration.

Your Choices & Rights

  • App Tracking Transparency — iOS asks you once whether to allow tracking. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • GDPR & US state consent — if you're in the EU, UK, Switzerland, or an applicable US state, FUSE shows a consent dialog before serving personalized ads. You can revisit your choices from the main menu's Settings.
  • Remove ads entirely — purchase FUSE Complete ($4.99) or the Founder Pack ($9.99, launch period only). Neither ever shows an ad prompt thereafter.
  • Right to delete — all your data is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all data.

Data Storage & Retention

  • All gameplay data (progress, settings, owned themes and signatures, session history) is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard localStorage / UserDefaults. Nothing is uploaded.
  • Aggregated, anonymous analytics buffered on your device may be sent to a FUSE analytics endpoint. These never include identifiers that could tie back to you, and are retained at most 90 days.
  • Ad interaction records are handled by Google AdMob under Google's retention policies.

Data Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not transfer your information to any party other than the service providers listed in "Third-Party Services" above, and only as needed to operate the features you use.

Children's Privacy

FUSE is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

Legal Compliance

FUSE is designed to comply with:

  • The EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.
  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).
  • US state privacy laws including those of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, and Texas.
  • The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • Apple App Store review guidelines, including App Tracking Transparency.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be surfaced in the app itself on the next launch.
